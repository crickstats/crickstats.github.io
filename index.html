FAQ & Help

FAQ

Is the program free?

It's free as long as you use it for personal purposes only.

Why does it come up with error message on open?

It is likely that you haven't got .NET Framework 4 installed on your computer. However, if the problem persists please take a screenshot and contact us with a description of the program and a screenshot attached.

Do you provide technical assitance with downloading the program and installing it on my computer?

We do not and you'll have to resolve all issues to do with your computer by yourself. However, you are more than welcome to report bugs by emailing us.

Why did you create the program?

The program started as an idea for the Monash CGBC IT Challenge Competition. We thought that this will be useful for cricket fans who want to look up players' stats while without an Internet Connection.
